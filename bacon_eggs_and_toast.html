Bacon, Eggs and Toast

Start your morning off the right way with a quick and easy breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Three large eggs
  • Four rashes of bacon
  • Two slices of bread
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Arrange bacon on a parchment-lined baking sheet and place in a preheated 200 degrees oven for ten minutes
  2. Crack the eggs and half of the butter into a saucepan and stir constantly over a stovetop set to medium high.
  3. If you for any reason need to stop stirring remove saucepan from heat. This is to keep the egg curd as small as possible
  4. When eggs approach desired consistency, remove from the heat and season. Continue to cook to desired consistency and then add remainig butter
  5. Smear eggs over toasted bread and garnish with the bacon
