Sausage Stew (Menemem)

Tastes like home

Ingredients

  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chorizo
  • Bell pepper
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cook onions and garlic until translucent and add bell pepper
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, spices and chorizo, raise to a simmer and then lower heat
  3. After twenty minutes, crack eggs into stew and mix in grated cheese
  4. Serve with bread
