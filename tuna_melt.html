Tuna Melt

An easy to make snack

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Mayonaise
  • Garlic powder
  • Cayenne
  • Olive Oil
  • Cheese
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Mix tuna, mayo and spices in small bow and mix until combined. Add olive oil and continue to mix until velvetty
  2. Toast bread and preheat oven
  3. Layer the tuna spread on toast and top with cheese, cook in oven until cheese is bubbly and slightly brown
