Chicken Stew (Coq au Vin)

This dish is rich in flavour and easy to make

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Mushrooms
  • Bacon
  • Tomato Paste
  • Red wine (can substitute with red wine vinegar in a pinch
  • Chicken stock

Steps

  1. Chop bacon into small pieces and cook off fat in pan. Set aside cooked bacon for later
  2. Cook chicken in the bacon fat. Most important is to get some good colour on the chicken
  3. Set aside chicken alongside the bacon, and place in pan onion and garlic. Cook until translucent, and add mushrooms
  4. Add tomato paste and coat vegetables well, then deglaze the pan with red wine
  5. When red wine is reduced add chicken stock and bring to a simmer
  6. Reintroduce chicken and bacon and reduce heat. Leave to cook for upwards of thirty minutes
